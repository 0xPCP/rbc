About Cycling Clubs

One platform for riders and the clubs that bring them together.

The Problem We're Solving

If you've ever tried to find group rides in your area, you know the drill: every club has its own website (or no website at all), ride info lives in old Facebook posts, and half the links are broken. You end up missing rides simply because you didn't know they existed.

On the club side, the problem is just as real. Most cycling clubs are run by volunteers who want to focus on riding, not maintaining a website. Ride calendars get out of date, signup tracking happens in spreadsheets, and new members have a hard time getting connected.

Cycling Clubs exists to fix both of those problems.

For Riders

🔍
Discover local clubs

Search by city, zip code, or browse the map to find clubs near you — from neighborhood beginner groups to fast race-pace teams.

📅
One calendar for all your clubs

Join multiple clubs and see every upcoming ride in a single dashboard. No more checking five different websites.

⛅
Weather at a glance

Each ride shows a live weather forecast so you can decide what to pack — or whether to pack it in — before you leave home.

✅
Simple ride sign-ups

Sign up for rides in one click and see who else is coming. Add to your calendar or download the GPX route before you head out.

For Clubs

🌐
A real club website, free

Your club gets a clean, mobile-friendly home page with your branding, description, contact info, and upcoming rides — no web development required.

🗓️
Easy ride management

Add recurring or one-off rides in minutes. Set pace, distance, meeting location, and route. Cancel rides with a reason that goes straight to members.

📋
Waivers and sign-up tracking

Manage your annual club waiver and see exactly who has signed up for each ride — no separate form tools or spreadsheets needed.

🚴
Strava club integration

Link your Strava club and recent group ride activities automatically appear on your club's home page, keeping it fresh without extra effort.

Get Started

Create a free account, find clubs near you, and start signing up for rides. If you run a club and want to get listed, reach out — we'd love to help you get set up.
