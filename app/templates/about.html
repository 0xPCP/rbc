About Reston Bike Club

Who we are and how we ride.

Our Story

Reston Bike Club has been bringing cyclists together in Northern Virginia for over two decades. What started as a small group of neighbors heading out on Sunday mornings has grown into a vibrant community of riders who share a passion for cycling and the outdoors.

We believe cycling is better together. Whether you're chasing KOMs or just enjoying the view, RBC has a group for you.

Weekly Rides

We host regular group rides throughout the week. Rides are organized by pace category — A through D — so everyone can find a comfortable group. Routes typically start in the Reston area and explore the back roads of Loudoun, Fairfax, and Fauquier counties, as well as the W&OD Trail corridor.

Check the ride calendar for upcoming departures, locations, and details.

Membership

Membership is free and open to anyone. Create an account to sign up for rides, see who else is coming, and share your ride media with the club.
