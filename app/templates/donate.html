About the founder

Hi, I'm Phil Porter.

I'm a cyclist in Northern Virginia who spends most of my time on the road, with the occasional gravel ride mixed in. I love group rides, but finding them, especially when you're new to an area, can be harder than it should be.

I built Paceline to make it easier for riders to discover local clubs and group rides, and to give clubs a simpler way to manage their online presence. Right now this is a passion project, and I'm paying for everything out of pocket.

Donations help cover hosting infrastructure and the AI agents I use to develop, test, and improve new features. Please send feedback below. - Phil
